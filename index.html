some HIDDEN GEMS of INDIA

India is home to a number of hidden temple gems, where Art and Culture and are inspiring Masterpieces.. These sacred sites hold a rich History and Spirituality. Each Temple offers a captivating narrative of India's diverse cultural Heritage.

Featured on the week

  • Khajuraho's Temples
    Khajuraho

    The Khajuraho Group of Monuments are a group of Hindu and Jain Temples in Chhatarpur, Madhya Pradesh. It is listed as UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1986

  • Hampi Temple
    Hampi

    Hampi is an Ancient Village in South Indian state of Karnataka. It is dotted with numerous ruined temple complexes from the Vijaynagar Empire.

  • Ajanta Caves
    Maharashtra

    These are 29 rock-cut Buddhist Cave Monuments dating from 2nd century BCE to about 480 CE in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. These caves are UNESCO World Heritage Site

  • Shri Tulja Bhawani Temple
    Maharashtra

    Hindu Temple dedicated to Goddess Parvati and is considered to be one of the 51 Shakti Pithas. This temple was built in 12th Century CE.

  • Shri Naina Devi Temple
    Bilaspur

    It is dedicated to Goddess Sati. It is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas. It is beleived that 'eyes' of Mother Sati fell here.

  • Maa Sharda Devi Maihar - Shaktipeeth
    Mirzapur

    It is the abode of Goddess Sharda devi on Trikut Mountain, 5 Kilometers from Stana. Devotees had to climb 1063 steps to reach the Temple.
